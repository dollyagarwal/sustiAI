Executive Summary

The Visionary Group's Sustainability Summit event generated a total of 85,167.17 kg CO₂e. The event hosted 364 attendees across 50,000 sq ft, resulting in an emissions intensity of 233.98 kg CO₂e per attendee. Scope 1 emissions, driven primarily by Venue Energy, Materials, and Travel, accounted for the largest share of the footprint, while Catering was a notable contributor within Scope 3.

Key Insights

  • Total Emissions: The Sustainability Summit generated 85,167.17 kg CO₂e, equivalent to approximately 85.17 metric tonnes.
  • Scope 1 Dominance: Scope 1 emissions represent the majority of the footprint, totaling 65,234.58 kg CO₂e (76.60%).
  • Scope 3 Contribution: Scope 3 emissions contributed 19,932.59 kg CO₂e (23.40%) to the overall total.
  • Emission Hotspots: The top three emission categories are Venue Energy (21.65%), Materials (20.78%), and Travel (17.65%), collectively accounting for over 60% of total emissions.
  • Significant Scope 3 Category: Catering is a substantial Scope 3 contributor, responsible for 14,348.38 kg CO₂e (16.85%).
  • Intensity Metrics: The event's emissions intensity was 233.98 kg CO₂e per attendee and 1.70 kg CO₂e per square foot.

Recommendations

  • Optimize Venue Energy and Materials: Implement energy efficiency measures at the venue and prioritize the use of low-carbon, recycled, or locally sourced materials for event infrastructure to significantly reduce Scope 1 emissions.
  • Promote Sustainable Travel & Accommodation: Encourage attendees and staff to use public transport or carpooling, offer hybrid event options to reduce long-distance travel, and select accommodations with verified sustainability certifications.
  • Green Catering Practices: Work with catering partners to reduce food waste, increase plant-based options, and source ingredients from local, sustainable producers to mitigate Scope 3 catering emissions.
